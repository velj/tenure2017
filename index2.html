Dear Dr. Gray and Dr. Berryman,

Please accept this electronic portfolio as my official request to become a tenured faculty member. My experience teaching at McHenry County College has allowed me to grow as an educator and as a professional in the field of early childhood education.

These past few years have been rewarding, meaningful, and overall an amazing experience. I love being a faculty member at MCC, because I truly believe in the vision, mission, and philosophy of the college. Not only do I consider my work with students important, I also consider it valuable. My past teaching experiences and commitment to early childhood education provide me with a firm foundation to be an effective tenured faculty member.

I consider myself a lifelong learner and I believe that my students encourage me to better myself every day. In the documents provided in my electronic portfolio, you will find evidence of my dedication to students and to the field of early childhood education. I have arranged this portfolio into the areas of subject matter, pedagogy, assessment, and professionalism. I have chosen documentation that I believe reflects my philosophy, as well as my commitment to the college.

Sincerely,
Melissa Veljasevic, M.S.
